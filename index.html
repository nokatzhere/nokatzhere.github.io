Mobilize

Plan activities with ease

  The tooling you need

Mobilize is a mobile application that makes it painlessly easy to plan group activities. Mobilize makes it easy to answer the questions:

  • Who's going?
  • Where are we going?
  • What time should we get there?
  • How are we going to get there?
  • How are we going to pay?
